Projects

BMP

BMP (Build My PC) is my end-to-end product that helps people choose the most compatible PC build for their needs. I built the compatibility checker from scratch, collected and cleaned hardware data, designed the database, and developed both the front-end and back-end. I also created a recommendation system using machine learning and data science to suggest builds to users.

Motion2Music

My thesis project: an AI prototype that extracts emotion, rhythm, and motion features from movement data and generates original music tailored to a specific dance or action. The goal is to make game music interactive and adaptive to a character's movements in real time.

Library Website

My first finished product for a real client: a school library booking system with a database-backed website. I worked as part of a team and contributed to building and delivering the full solution.

Game Engine (HTML5)

A small 2D game engine built with pure JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I made it while learning web development and exploring game architecture. To validate it, I built playable demos like Pong and Breakout on top of the engine.

Backgammon 2.0

A modern 3D take on Backgammon with added strategy and mini-games (e.g., tic-tac-toe, maze, and more). The goal was to reduce pure luck and make the experience more skill-based and engaging.

Portfolio Website

ICPC - Qualified for European Regionals
